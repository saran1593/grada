Grad Architects is led by Principal Architect Vishnu Priya, who brings rich experience in Residential, Commercial, and Industrial projects. Before founding the firm, she worked with leading practices such as A&E Architects and Engineers, Chennai.

Since its inception, the firm has grown steadily, delivering projects that balance guidelines, aesthetics, and context. Our dedicated team ensures personalized care, from initial planning to detailed design and on-site execution.

As a certified professional in Mud Architecture and a passionate advocate of Green Architecture, Vishnu Priya's philosophy drives Grad Architects' focus on sustainability, innovation, and timeless design.
